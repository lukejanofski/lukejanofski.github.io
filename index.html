Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Mayo 2 Tablespoons
Unsalted Butter 1 Tablespoon
Cheese 4 slices
Black pepper Choice

Step 1: Place bread on a cutting board and spread mayonnaise over top side of each
Step 2: Heat a small skillet (nonstick, ideally) over medium
Step 3: Slide in half of butter
Step 4: When it melts, place 1 slice of bread, mayonnaise side down, in skillet; top with cheese; season with pepper. Top with second slice of bread, mayonnaise side up.
Step 5: When underside is golden brown, about 4 minutes, turn sandwich and add remaining butter to skillet. Press down on sandwich to encourage even browning and to help melt cheese—be gentle, don’t smash it.
Step 6: Cook until second side is golden brown and cheese is melted.

Fruit Salad

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8-10
Ingredients Quantity
Honey 1/4 Cup
Orange Juice 1/4 Cup
Quartered Strawberries 1 lb.
Zest of Lemon 1 Lemon
Bluberries 6 oz
Raspberries 6 oz
Kiwi Sliced&Peeled 3
Peeled and Wedged Orange 1
Apples Peeled and Chopped 2
Grapes 2 cups
Mango Peeled/Chopped 1

Step 1: In a small bowl whisk together honey, orange juice, and lemon zest. Add fruit to a large bowl and pour over dressing, tossing gently to combine. Chill until ready to serve.

Strawberry Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 Cup
Strawberry Greek Yogurt 1/2 Cup
Frozen Cut Bannana 1
Frozen Strawberries 1

Step 1: Place ingredients in blender in the order written.
Step 2: Puree until smooth, add a bit more milk or water if smoothie is too thick to blend. Taste and add a bit of honey if desired to sweeten.
